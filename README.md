--Electric Vehicle Market Analysis in India --

A Power BI–based analytical study of India’s electric vehicle landscape, focusing on sales trends, regional adoption, charging infrastructure, and market growth patterns.

**Overview**

This project provides a detailed analysis of the Electric Vehicle (EV) ecosystem in India using Power BI.
The dashboard presents insights on market adoption, state-wise trends, EV categories, and the overall growth trajectory of the Indian EV sector.
The visualizations help understand how consumer adoption, infrastructure expansion, and government policies influence EV growth.

**Key Features**

State-wise EV registration and adoption analysis

Category-wise EV distribution (2W, 3W, 4W, buses)

Year-wise and month-wise EV sales trends

Charging station distribution and growth

Regional comparison through maps and bar charts

Trend analysis and insights curated for decision-making

**Tech Stack**

Power BI for data modelling, cleaning, and visualization

Excel/CSV as data sources
